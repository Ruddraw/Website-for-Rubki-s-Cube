Welcome to the world of SpeedCubing. I am AFIF ASAD RUDDRAW, a hobbiest speedcuber. I created this website to show the rubik's cube I use as my main cube. I chosed these cubes based on their performance, feels, customization ability, and price. Hpoe you like it.




Valk2 M is the first 2x2 produced in the iconic Valk line. Coming in at a slightly larger than the normal 51mm, this puzzle is solidly built with a stable and more substantial feel.The magnet positions on this 2x2 are unlike any seen on the market. Rather than the typical corner-only magnet layout, the Valk2 M uses a corner/edge magnet layout resembling that of a 3x3. This gives the puzzle a smaller magnetic radius making the feel more consistent throughout the turn.

The GAN 11 M Pro is GAN's upcoming 2020 flagship 3x3 magnetic speed cube. Built with an omnidirectional magnetic core, this cube delivers a unique, enhanced magnetic feel that is also easy to adjust.GAN's new omnidirectional magnetic core is the industry's first corner-core magnetic positioning system. In addition to the 48 traditionally placed magnets inside the cube's pieces, the GAN 11 M Pro features 8 pairs of magnets stemming from the core to each corner base. This magnetic positioning system creates a consistent magnetic feel that is strong without an aggressive snap.The GAN 11 M Pro features the same dual adjustment system as the popular GAN 356 XS. Use the included adjustment tool to set elasticity to 6 different levels. Tension by hand to 4 different settings with the smaller blue nut.

The X-Man Design Spark 7x7 M is a magnetic 7x7 designed specifically for speed solving. It features a smaller, lighter design with a medium to strong magnetic feel. These features make the Spark exceptionally stable, reliable, and easy to turn. Max Park used this puzzle to set the 1:40.89 single and 1:46.57 average 7x7 world records in August 2019 and January 2020 respectively.

The X-Man Wingy Magnetic Skewb is the latest skewb release from X-Man Designs and the first concave skewb to hit the market. The Wingy boasts improved grip and control due to its innovative concave design, and it features 24 magnets with adjustable positioning for an even more custom experience. Sturdy and reliable, the X-Man Wingy is a strong choice for cubers interested in skewb. Used by Andrew Huang to set the 0.93 second skewb single world record in July 2019
